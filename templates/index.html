90-Day Roadmap

Day {{ days_in }} — Phase {{ phase }}: {{ phase_name }}
1

Build Engine

Days 1-30

Goal: Consistency + Data Collection. Not virality. Not money. Data.

  • 5-10 videos per day (150 in 30 days minimum)
  • 10 hooks written daily
  • Metrics logged daily
  • 1 format tested per week
  • Week 1: Test 3 hook styles, 2 bg visuals, 2 voice styles
  • Week 2: Double down on best hook format
  • Week 3: Improve average watch time, add pattern interrupts
  • Week 4: Identify top 20%, reverse engineer, clone structure
KPI: 150+ uploads, clear retention data
2

Optimize & Scale

Days 31-60

Goal: Refine what works. Kill what doesn't.

  • 8-15 videos per day
  • If retention < 50% at 3s, hook is trash — rewrite
  • Choose 2 winning formats + 1 backup. Cut everything else.
  • Batch production: Mon scripts, Tue record, Wed-Thu edit, Fri schedule
  • Weekend: light analytics + strategy only
KPI: 400+ uploads, 1 video 50k+ views, repeatable format
3

Multiply & Monetize

Days 61-90

Goal: Scale + activate revenue streams.

  • Consistent retention above 60%
  • Activate affiliate links + digital downloads
  • Build email funnel + paid newsletter
  • Brand email outreach — don't wait for deals
  • Clone format into secondary niche
  • Hire editor / script contractor
KPI: 1,000+ uploads, 5-20k followers, monetization activated
